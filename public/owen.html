Key Notes

  • Awake windows are short (~60–90 min including the feed). Watch for sleepy cues — yawning, fussing, looking away — and put him down before he's overtired.
  • Bottle prep: mix formula per the can first, then add breastmilk. Warm in a bowl of warm water — never the microwave. Swirl, don't shake hard.
  • Breastmilk handling: thawed/refrigerated milk used within 24 hrs; don't refreeze. A combined bottle: use within ~1 hr once he starts drinking; discard leftovers 1 hr after a feed begins.
  • Always back to sleep, on a firm flat surface — no loose blankets, pillows, or toys.
  • Burp midway through and after each feed.
  • Diapers: 6–8 wet diapers/day means he's eating enough. Total intake ~32 oz/day.
  • Night wakings to feed are expected — don't try to push him through.
