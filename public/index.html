Pit Stops for Norah

Playgrounds picked for an almost-3-year-old — soft surfaces, shade, bathrooms nearby, and all a short hop off the route. Tap any stop to see details and directions.

Day 01 · Amarillo
AMBUCS Playground · John Ward Memorial Park
1100 Wallace Blvd · ★ 4.7
▾
The big one. Splash pad, zipline, merry-go-round, swings & slides on a soft faux-grass surface. Lake with ducks to feed, shaded picnic tables, tons of parking. A little south of I-40 — worth the detour after 3.5 hrs in the van.

Quiet little playground with picnic tables, right off the highway. Classic PBJ-and-run stop before the push to Santa Fe.

Day 02 · Pagosa Springs
Yamaguchi Park
684 S 5th St · ★ 4.7
▾
Better than the Riverwalk for her age — has a separate toddler playground alongside the big-kid one, plus open room to run. River, clean bathrooms, shade ramadas, a couple miles off the touristy strip so it's calmer.

Day 02 · Durango
Santa Rita Park
111 S Camino Del Rio · ★ 4.7
▾
Big new playground with large slides, right by your lunch spot. Restrooms, covered tables, river access. Heads up: sits right on the river — water's fast and cold, so keep eyes on her near the edge.
